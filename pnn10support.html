HOW TO USE PNN10 CI

1 - Install PNN10 CI from the App store

2 - Give permission to read contacts. (We do not collect your data, the application works without internet.)

3 - On the main menu, you have 5 views. 3 for your mobile numbers from each of the telephone operators in Cote d`Ivoire, 1 for landline numbers and 1 for all contacts together

4 - You can view the numbers of your telephone directory which match with the filters of Cote d`Ivoire

5 - clicking on a contact allows you to see it better

6 - Keeping the contact a little longer displays a menu that allows you to migrate all that you have selected

7 - click on migrate to get the confirmation message before actually starting your migration

8 - If you want to migrate only one contact, there is a context menu on the contact line materializing not 3 small dots.

9 - click on the three small dots and select the action to modify contact.
