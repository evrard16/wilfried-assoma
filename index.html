PRIVACY POLICY

About section:

Following the announcement of the change of Ivory Coast phone numbers, we have decided to offer this application for free to allow anyone who wishes to change their Ivory Coast phone numbers directly in the new format of it which comes into force on January 31, 2021.

In fact, the data is used locally to make it possible to propose the new nomenclature of Ivory Coast telephone numbers.

The application does not connect to the internet, all the processing is done locally

Collection and use of information:

Faced with the development of new communication tools, it is necessary to pay particular attention to the protection of privacy. PNN10 CI does not collect your data.

We pay particular attention to this migration. For this, we recommend that you back up your contacts before the start of the migration, so that you can restore them as you wish. PNN10 CI is in no way responsible for any damage caused to your phone.
